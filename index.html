Introduction

Community Learning Passport, operated by Dottie Rose Consulting LLC d/b/a The Dot. Consulting (“we,” “us,” or “our”), provides the Community Learning Passport mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use the App.

Who this App is for

The App is designed for families. Accounts are created and used by parents or legal guardians who are at least 18 years old. By creating an account or using the App, you represent that you are the account holder and are responsible for information submitted through your account, including information about your family, learning activities, written reflections, and any photos you choose to upload.

Information about children or family activities may appear in the App, but it is provided through a parent or guardian’s account rather than through a separate child account or login.

Information we collect

Account information: We collect the email address, password, and related authentication information used to create and sign in to your account. Passwords are handled securely by our authentication service provider and are not available to us in readable form.

Profile information: We collect information you provide in your profile, such as the parent or guardian’s first and last name, family nickname, school or organization name, School Access information, and preferences such as whether photo-sharing features are enabled.

Learning activity information: We collect information you enter as you use the App, including monthly activity choices, book or checklist selections, written reflections, answers to prompts, check-in status, completion status, badges, rewards, and overall program progress.

Photos: If you choose to upload photos, the image files are stored through our service provider and associated with your account and the month or activity you selected. Where sharing options are available, you may choose whether a photo remains private or is shared for an approved school, community, promotional, or public purpose.

Purchase and access information: If you purchase Individual Family Access through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, we may receive information about the purchase, including the product purchased, transaction identifier, purchase date, store platform, purchase status, and access-entitlement status.

We use RevenueCat to verify purchases and manage access to paid features. RevenueCat may process transaction and entitlement information received from Apple or Google. We do not receive or store your complete credit card number, debit card number, bank-account number, or other full payment credentials.

School Access information: If you receive access through a participating school or organization, we may collect and process the school or organization name, School Access code, code-redemption status, access type, access eligibility, and information needed to associate the account with the appropriate school-sponsored program.

Technical and session data: To keep you signed in and provide the App’s functionality, the App may store session-related information on your device using local device storage. Records of your access type, purchase entitlement, School Access status, and photo-sharing choices may also be stored locally or in our backend systems.

We may receive limited technical information needed to operate and secure the App, such as device type, operating system, App version, authentication events, and error information.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create, authenticate, maintain, and secure your account and profile.
  • Provide the App’s features, including monthly learning activities, check-ins, written reflections, progress tracking, badges, rewards, and completion records.
  • Save and display photos you choose to upload and apply the sharing preferences associated with those photos.
  • Verify purchases, restore eligible purchases, determine your access entitlement, and provide Individual Family Access or School Access features.
  • Redeem and validate School Access codes and associate participating accounts with the appropriate school or organization.
  • Support school or program reporting where applicable, such as helping authorized school or program personnel determine whether participating families completed program activities.
  • Respond to support requests, privacy requests, account-deletion requests, and questions about purchases or access.
  • Operate, maintain, troubleshoot, secure, and improve the App and comply with applicable legal requirements.

Marketing and promotional use of photos: We will only use an uploaded photo for marketing, promotional, community, or public purposes when the applicable sharing option has been selected or appropriate consent has been provided. Please only share photos publicly that you are comfortable allowing others to view.

We do not use purchase information for targeted advertising. We do not sell your personal information or complete payment information.

The current App build does not include dedicated third-party advertising or analytics software development kits designed to track your activity across other companies’ apps or websites.

Purchases and payment processing

Individual Family Access may be offered as a one-time in-app purchase through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. The price and terms displayed at the time of purchase are controlled by the applicable app store.

Apple or Google processes the payment using the payment method connected to your app-store account. Their processing of your payment information is governed by their own terms and privacy policies. We do not directly process or store your complete payment-card information.

We use RevenueCat to receive confirmation of eligible purchases, manage access entitlements, support purchase restoration, and determine whether an account should have access to paid App features.

Refunds, payment disputes, and other app-store billing matters are generally handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store according to the applicable store’s policies.

School Access may be provided separately through a participating school or organization. A user receiving School Access may not be required to make an individual in-app purchase. School Access may be subject to the school or organization’s participation period, code validity, and program terms.

How we share information

We may share information with service providers that process information on our behalf and help us provide, secure, and maintain the App. These providers may use the information only as needed to provide their services or as otherwise permitted by law.

If you participate through a school or organization, authorized school or program personnel may receive limited information relating to program participation, such as the family or account identifier, school association, completion status, badges, progress, or other information needed to administer the program.

Photos are not shared publicly or used for promotional purposes unless the applicable sharing option has been selected or consent has been provided.

We may disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with applicable law, respond to lawful requests, protect the security and integrity of the App, enforce our terms, or protect the rights and safety of users or others.

If the App or its operating business is involved in a merger, acquisition, reorganization, financing, or transfer of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.

Service providers

We use trusted service providers to host, operate, and support the App.

Supabase: We use Supabase for services such as account authentication, database hosting, account and progress storage, and file storage for uploaded content such as photos.

RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to verify in-app purchases, manage purchase entitlements, restore eligible purchases, and determine access to paid App features.

Apple App Store and Google Play Store: Apple and Google process in-app purchase payments and provide transaction-confirmation information. We do not collect or store your complete payment-card or bank-account information.

Expo and React Native: The App is built using Expo and React Native and uses standard device and platform capabilities, such as allowing you to select or capture photos. Session and access information may be stored on your device using local storage required for sign-in and App functionality.

These providers process information according to their own terms and privacy practices and, where applicable, on our behalf to provide the services described in this Privacy Policy.

Data security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information handled through the App and our service providers.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee that information will never be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed through unauthorized means.

Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active and as long as reasonably necessary to provide the App, administer purchases or School Access, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and protect the App and its users.

When you delete your account through the App, we permanently remove or initiate deletion of your account credentials, profile information, learning progress, reflections, and uploaded photos from our active systems, subject to limited retention in backups, transaction records, security logs, or other records where retention is required or permitted by law.

Deleting your App account does not delete transaction records maintained independently by Apple, Google, or another payment provider. Those records are governed by the applicable provider’s retention practices.

Your choices

You can update profile information and certain preferences in the App, including photo-sharing preferences where those options are available.

You can choose not to upload photos. Where photo-visibility choices are available, you may select the applicable private or sharing option before submitting the photo.

You may restore an eligible prior purchase through the restore-purchases option available in the App.

You can permanently delete your account and associated data in the App: Open the App, go to Home, tap Profile, scroll to Danger Zone, and tap Delete Account. Confirm the warning and enter your password to permanently delete your account.

See our Delete Your Account page for full instructions.

You may also contact us using the information below to request access to or correction of personal information associated with your account.

Children’s privacy

The App is designed to be used through an account created and controlled by a parent or legal guardian who is at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly permit children to create separate accounts or directly submit personal information without the involvement of a parent or guardian.

We seek to limit the collection of information that directly identifies children. Parents and guardians should avoid including unnecessary personal information about a child in family nicknames, written reflections, uploaded photos, or other user-submitted content.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App in a way that does not match this design, please contact us. We will review the request and take appropriate steps, which may include deleting the information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the App, our information practices, our service providers, or applicable requirements.

We will post the updated policy with a revised effective date or last-updated date. If a change is material, we may provide additional notice or request consent where required by law.
