Community Learning Passport lets you permanently delete your account and associated data directly in the mobile app. Deletion is final and cannot be undone.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Sign in to Community Learning Passport on your device.
  2. From Home, tap Profile.
  3. Scroll to the Danger zone section.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Read the warning, check the confirmation box, enter your password, and tap Delete permanently.

This step is final. Once confirmed, your account and data listed below are permanently deleted.

Data that is deleted

  • Your account and sign-in email
  • Profile information (parent name, family nickname, school, preferences)
  • Learning activity data (monthly progress, reflections, checklists)
  • Photos you uploaded through the App

Data that may be retained

We do not keep a copy of your account profile, progress, or photos after successful in-app deletion. Our infrastructure provider may retain encrypted backups for a limited time as part of standard operations; those backups are not used to restore deleted accounts except where required by law.
